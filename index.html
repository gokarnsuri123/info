Project 01 / 03

VarunNetra
AI Anomaly Detection ¬∑ Water Pipelines

Water loss through illegal tapping and undetected leakage is a critical public infrastructure crisis. VarunNetra detects anomalies the moment they occur ‚Äî replacing slow, expensive manual inspection.

Problem
No Real-Time Visibility

Municipalities lose millions of litres daily to pipeline failures. Manual inspection is too slow and costly for large urban networks.

Solution
Continuous AI Monitoring

Processes flow and pressure sensor data in real time using ML to distinguish normal operation from tapping and leakage ‚Äî with instant alerts.

Technical Approach
Pattern Learning Per Segment

ML models trained on per-segment sensor baselines detect statistical deviations ‚Äî filtering noise while flagging genuine anomalies for operators.

Anomaly Detection Sensor Data ML Models Real-Time
Impact
Smart City Ready

Integrates with public utility networks and smart city frameworks ‚Äî reducing non-revenue water loss and giving governments actionable infrastructure intelligence.

Project 02 / 03
üìÑ Patent Filed
SBE
Smart Bus Ecosystem ¬∑ Overload Detection

Overloaded public buses are a persistent safety hazard in India's transit systems. SBE uses AI to detect passenger overloading in real time and trigger preventive action before it becomes a crisis.

Problem
Unsafe Overloading Undetected

Buses routinely breach safety thresholds with no automated detection. Drivers rely on manual judgment ‚Äî often under pressure to proceed anyway.

Solution
AI-Based Load Monitoring

Monitors passenger load dynamically, flagging overloading events in real time and alerting operators, drivers, and transport authorities automatically.

Technical Approach
Sensor Fusion + Classification

Weight and occupancy inputs processed through a trained model identifying safe, near-limit, and overload states ‚Äî enabling graduated alerts, not binary triggers.

Sensor Fusion Load Classification Transit Safety Patent Pending
Impact
Safer Transit at Scale

Deployable across city bus fleets ‚Äî reduces accident risk, supports regulatory compliance, and gives operators fleet-wide safety telemetry.

Project 03 / 03
üî¨ Active Development
VigilDrive
AI Drowsiness Detection ¬∑ Road Safety

Driver fatigue causes thousands of road accidents each year. Vigil Drive uses computer vision AI to detect early signs of drowsiness in real time and intervene before an incident occurs.

Problem
Fatigue: A Silent Road Hazard

Drowsy driving accounts for a significant share of fatal accidents in India. No affordable real-time detection system exists for commercial vehicles.

Solution
Real-Time Drowsiness Alerting

Monitors facial landmarks and eye movement through a camera feed, using AI to detect drowsiness indicators and alert the driver immediately.

Technical Approach
Computer Vision + Behavioural AI

Tracks eye aspect ratio, blink frequency, and head pose in real time using lightweight models designed to run on embedded vehicle hardware.

Computer Vision Eye Tracking Edge AI Road Safety
Impact
Deployable Across Fleets

Targeted at truck fleets, bus operators, and ride-share platforms ‚Äî affordable, AI-powered fatigue monitoring with minimal hardware requirements.
