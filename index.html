Overloaded public buses are a persistent safety hazard in India's transit systems ‚Äî causing accidents, accelerating vehicle wear, and creating liability for operators. SBE uses AI to detect passenger overloading in real time and trigger preventive action before it becomes a crisis.

Problem

Overcrowded buses breach safety thresholds routinely, with no automated detection mechanism. Drivers and conductors rely on manual judgment ‚Äî often under pressure to proceed anyway.

Weight and occupancy sensor inputs are processed through a trained classification model that identifies safe, near-limit, and overload states ‚Äî enabling graduated alerts rather than binary triggers.

Driver fatigue and drowsiness cause thousands of road accidents each year ‚Äî particularly in long-haul trucking, bus operations, and night driving. Vigil Drive uses AI-based computer vision to detect early signs of drowsiness in real time and intervene before an incident occurs.

Problem

Drowsy driving accounts for a significant percentage of fatal road accidents in India. There is no widely deployed, affordable real-time detection system for commercial or public vehicle operators.

Vigil Drive monitors facial landmarks and eye movement patterns through a camera feed, using AI to detect drowsiness indicators and immediately alert the driver before loss of control.

Technical Approach
Computer Vision + Behavioural AI

The system tracks eye aspect ratio, blink frequency, and head pose in real time using lightweight computer vision models ‚Äî designed to run efficiently on embedded hardware in vehicles.
